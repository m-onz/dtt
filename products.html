TELESCOPE

PORTABLE DRONE DETECTION DEVICE

TELESCOPE is a self contained and portable drone detection device combining radio, audio and optical detection capabilites that does not need to connect to the internet in order to operate. The TELESCOPE device has two microphone inputs that accept a range of different microphone peripherals. It also has an inbuilt infra red camera with automated drone detection capable of working in day and night.

OMNI DIRECTIONAL MICROPHONE ATTACHMENT

AUDIO DRONE DETECTION SENSOR

Completely water proof omni directional microphone attachment for the TELESCOPE drone detection device. This microphone is intended to be used with standard satellite dishes to become an effective parabolic microphone with a range of up to 500 meters.

SHOTGUN MICROPHONE ATTACHMENT

AUDIO DRONE DETECTION SENSOR

This shotgun micriphone detachment provides precise audio recording with sound isolation up around 200 meters.
