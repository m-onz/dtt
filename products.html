Why use TELESCOPE?

* Defeat location and advertisement tracking

* Access the internet from a secure environment or network without exposing it to risk

* Get VPN functionality with a web browser (no VPN required)

* You get complete isolation between TELESCOPES to avoid all pivoting and lateral attacks

TELESCOPE is not a VPN, its a private browser or application running in an isolated server. It effectively protects you against spyware, malware and location tracking with a security model that can be easily explained to humans. You can integrate TELESCOPE within your network infrastucture or use our completely managed service to save on infrastucture costs.
